Programs

The BizarBots are affiliated with a multitude of organizations, first and foremostly FIRST and Vex. Our main focus is the FIRST Robotics Competition, and we are currently in the process of introducing our public school system to FTC (First Technology Challenge) and FLL(First Lego League) as well. Our team also competes in both Vex Robotics Competition: Vex EDR and Vex IQ.

The BizarBots have also created several self-run programs including: a STEM Summer Camp, free after-school tutoring, and other community outreach and charity events. We are continuously expanding our team and the programs we run. Check back here for more programs or click the links above to see the programs we currently run or participate in.
